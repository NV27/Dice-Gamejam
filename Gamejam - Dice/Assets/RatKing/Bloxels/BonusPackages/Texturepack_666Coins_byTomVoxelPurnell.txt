The 666 Coins texture pack was created by Tom "voxel" Purnell
https://thomaspurnell.com/
https://voxel.itch.io/

You can find 666 Coins here:
https://coolfriends.itch.io/666-coins

To install, double-click the Texturepack_666Coins_byTomVoxelPurnell.unitypackage and
import the whole content. Then press "Update Textures Only" in the Initialize menu.
Afterwards either press "Recreate Meshes" in Helpers, or "Recreate ALL Existing Meshes"
in Initialize in order to update the current bloxel meshes' texture coordinates.
